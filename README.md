# Financial Performance & Risk Analysis
## End-to-end financial data analysis using Python, SQL, and Power BI
## Dataset
The dataset contains company-level financial data including sales, profit, costs, business segments, and time-based attributes such as date and year.

## Data Cleaning & Processing
- Preserved raw data for reproducibility
- Cleaned formatted numeric fields (currency symbols, separators)
- Converted data into correct numeric and date formats
- Engineered key metrics such as profit margin and risk classification
- Generated an analysis-ready cleaned dataset
##  Key Insights
- The Government segment is the top profit contributor, accounting for ~62% of total profit
- Low Risk records generate the majority of sales and profit
- High Risk records show lower profit margins and are concentrated in specific segments
- Significant performance gap exists between top and bottom segments
## Power BI Dashboard
The Power BI dashboard provides an executive-level view of financial performance and risk.
**Dashboard Highlights:**
- Total Sales, Total Profit, and Average Profit Margin KPIs
- Profit contribution by business segment
- High Risk vs Low Risk distribution
- Sales vs Profit relationship by risk category

## Business Impact
- Identified key profit-driving segments
- Highlighted high-risk areas requiring margin improvement
- Enabled data-driven financial decision-making

- Building end-to-end analytics pipelines
- Translating data insights into business-focused storytelling
